Our Story

Local Favorites & Things to Do

If you have some extra time to explore the area, here are a few of our favorite spots near Newark and Sunol:

Coffee & Sweets

  • Suju's Coffee (Fremont/Newark): A beloved local staple with a cozy atmosphere, perfect for a morning latte or a quick caffeine fix.
  • Bun Appétit Donuts (Fremont): Gourmet, artisanal donuts with unique flavors like Guava Cheesecake and Ube. Be sure to get there early—they sell out fast!
  • See's Candies (Newark/Fremont): A legendary California chocolate shop founded in San Francisco. Stop by for a famous "free sample" and grab a box of Bordeaux or Scotchmallows.

Lunch & Dinner

  • Din Ding Dumpling House (Fremont): Famous for their handmade Xiao Long Bao (soup dumplings) and fresh noodles. A must-visit for authentic Shanghainese cuisine.
  • Happiness Hotpot (囍火锅): An authentic Chongqing-style hotpot experience. Be sure to visit the self-service sauce bar to create your own signature dip!
  • Ramen Nagi (Palo Alto/San Jose): Widely considered the best ramen in the Bay Area. You can fully customize your bowl, from the richness of the broth to the spice level.
  • Eataly Silicon Valley (Valley Fair): A massive three-story Italian marketplace. You can shop for artisanal pastas, grab a quick espresso or gelato, or enjoy a full meal at Terra, their beautiful rooftop restaurant.
  • Giorgio’s Italian Food (San Jose/Milpitas): A beloved South Bay institution for over 50 years. This family-owned favorite is famous for its cozy atmosphere and generous "family-style" portions of classic Italian comfort food and award-winning pizza.
  • In-N-Out Burger (Multiple Locations): A legendary California institution. Known for its simple menu and high-quality, never-frozen ingredients. Order a Double-Double and don't forget to ask for your burger or fries "Animal Style" from the not-so-secret menu!

Sightseeing & Nature

  • Lake Elizabeth (Central Park): A beautiful 80-acre lake in the heart of Fremont. It features a lovely 2-mile walking path around the water with views of the Mission Peak hills.
  • Sunol Regional Wilderness: A hidden gem for hiking. Highlights include "Little Yosemite," a scenic gorge with waterfalls and expansive views of the rolling hills.
  • Coyote Hills Regional Park: Beautiful walking trails with views of the San Francisco Bay and salt marshes.
  • Mission Peak: For the adventurous! A challenging 3-mile hike with a "famous" pole at the top and 360-degree views of the entire Bay Area.
  • Santana Row & Valley Fair: A premier outdoor destination in San Jose for high-end shopping, dining, and people-watching.

San Francisco Classics

  • Ghirardelli Square & Pier 39: Iconic waterfront destinations for world-famous chocolate sundaes, sea lions, and classic SF sourdough.
  • Golden Gate Park: A massive urban oasis home to the Japanese Tea Garden, the de Young Museum, and the California Academy of Sciences.
  • Union Square: The vibrant heart of downtown SF, famous for luxury shopping, historic theaters, and the iconic cable car turnaround at Powell Street.

California Day Trips

  • Napa Valley: About 1.5 hours north. We highly recommend Castello di Amorosa, an authentic 13th-century style Tuscan castle and winery.
  • Monterey & Carmel: About 1.5 hours south. Enjoy the world-class Monterey Bay Aquarium and the scenic 17-Mile Drive.
  • Yosemite National Park: Approximately 3.5 to 4 hours away. A world-renowned destination for breathtaking granite cliffs, giant sequoias, and waterfalls.
